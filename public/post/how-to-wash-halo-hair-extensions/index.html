How To Wash Halo Hair Extensions

May 31, 2022 · 9 min · Me

Best Answer

  1. Fill a sink with cool water
  2. Add a few drops of shampoo to the water
  3. Soak the hair in the water for a few minutes
  4. Rinse the hair in cool water
  5. Fill a sink with cool water and add a few drops of conditioner
  6. Soak the hair in the water for a few minutes
  7. Rinse the hair in cool water
  8. Gently squeeze the hair to remove excess water
  9. Wrap the hair in a towel and squeeze the water out
  10. Comb the hair with a wide tooth comb
  11. Place the hair in a ponytail holder or clip
  12. Place the hair on a towel and pat dry
