How To Wash Latex

Best Answer

  1. Use a mild soap and water to clean the latex.

  2. Rinse the latex with clean water.

  3. Dry the latex with a towel.

  4. Apply a thin layer of talcum powder to the latex.

  5. Store the latex in a cool, dry place.
